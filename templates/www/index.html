How does it work?

  • Subject is changing every 1-3 days
  • Post photos using Instagram based on current subject
  • To add photo to competition, manually add #instadaily hash-tag in caption or comment of given photo, or use InstaDaily which will do it for you
  • Until current subject runs out of time, people can vote on photos. Since it based on Instagram's 'like' feature, we also count votes made outside of InstaDaily app
  • Few hours after all votes are summed up and Leaderboards get updated, so you can check what's your current position
